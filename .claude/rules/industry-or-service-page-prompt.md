# Industry Or Service Page Prompt

Create a 2,500 to 4,000 word service page for `[COMPANY NAME]` serving the `[INDUSTRY / SERVICE CATEGORY]` using the complete master rule set below. Follow the required H1 and H2 structure exactly. Make the content fully unique with at least forty percent industry or service depth including pain points, buyer behavior, market psychology, objections, business model details, high intent keywords, operational challenges, economics, profit drivers, service delivery nuances, marketing challenges, competitive landscape, and demand patterns. Use a hybrid tone blending clarity, sharpness, premium authority, and direct response persuasion. Do not use an em dash. Keep readability at Grade 7 to 9. Keep keyword density under two percent. Use semantic variety with SXO structure and an LLM-friendly, AEO-ready layout. Include one FAQ section with ten to fifteen fully unique questions about the specific industry or service. Do not number them. Each answer should be snippet optimized and forty to sixty words. Do not overlap with any other page for any industry, service, or location. Leave space for internal links without adding links. Produce a clean, modern, conversion-focused narrative that positions `[COMPANY NAME]` as the leading choice in the `[INDUSTRY / SERVICE CATEGORY]`.

## Master Rule Set

### Uniqueness Requirements

Every page must be fully unique. No overlap with:

- any industry page
- any service page
- any location page
- any flagship page
- any previous draft
- any template

No reused phrasing.

At least forty percent of the content must be industry or service specific, covering pain points, buyer behavior, psychology, objections, business model mechanics, high intent keywords, operations, economics, profit levers, delivery challenges, competitor dynamics, and demand cycles.

### Mandatory Structure

Use this exact format, replacing the bracketed sections with the actual industry, service, or vertical:

- `H1: [Primary Service] for the [Industry]`
- `H2: Full Service Options for the [Industry]`
- `H2: Why the [Industry] Chooses [Company]`
- `H2: [Primary Service] Solutions for the [Industry]`
- `H2: Additional Services for the [Industry]`
- `H2: Results and Case Highlights for the [Industry]`
- `H2: Request Your [Industry] Service Assessment`
- `[Industry] and [Service] FAQ`

This structure works for local service businesses, national firms, manufacturing, B2B, SaaS, healthcare, trades, finance, professional services, retail, and more.

### Tone And Voice Rules

Use a hybrid tone: modern sharpness, clear authority, concise intelligence, and direct-response persuasion.

Rules:

- no em dash
- short paragraphs
- commercial intent baked in
- smart confident rhythm
- no hype
- precision and clarity over filler

### SEO, SXO, AEO, And LLM Standards

- keyword density under two percent
- use wide semantic variety
- include high-intent industry or service modifiers
- structure should be scannable
- all content must be LLM-friendly and AEO-ready
- use bold sparingly and only for emphasis
- maintain Grade 7 to 9 readability
- benefit-driven intros
- clarity over cleverness
- authority over adjectives

### Formatting Rules

- no em dash
- no numbered FAQ
- avoid duplicate sentence openings
- use H2 for main sections and H3 only when helpful
- clean spacing
- long-form narrative between 2,500 and 4,000 words

### Company Name Usage

Use `[COMPANY NAME]` naturally.

- do not overuse the name
- rotate approved name variations if the company has more than one version

### FAQ Rules

- one FAQ section total
- ten to fifteen questions
- all questions must be specific to the industry, service, or vertical
- answers must be snippet optimized with forty to sixty words
- no repetition across any other FAQs

### Depth And Word Count

Target 2,500 to 4,000 words depending on complexity.

Technical, legal, medical, and regulated fields should usually trend toward the higher end.

### Internal Linking

Leave space for internal link placement.

Do not insert actual links or build lists of pages.
