FINAL MASTER RULE SYSTEM FOR ALL LOCATION PAGES (Generalized Edition)

Zero Duplicate Content
Every location page must be one hundred percent original.
No swapping city names. No recycled intros. No reused FAQ answers. No template phrasing.
At least forty percent must be location specific.

Mandatory Structure for All Location Pages
Use this exact outline. The content must be custom every time.

H1: [Primary Service] in [City]
H2: Comprehensive [Service Category] Solutions in [City]

City specific intro

Why Businesses in [City] Choose [Company Name]

[City] [Service] Strategy and Execution

[Service Category] Options for [City] Clients

Custom [Service] Deliverables for [City]

Real [City] Case Studies and Client Results

About [City]
(Required section, placed before CTA or FAQ)

Get Your [City] [Service] Assessment

[City] [Service] FAQ
(One block only)

About City Section (Permanent Rule)
Every page must include an About City section that meets these requirements:

Reflects local culture, geography, lifestyle, economy, landmarks, neighborhoods, and character
One hundred percent original every time
Grade 7 to 9 readability
Smart, clean writing style
No hype or filler
No em dash
One hundred to two hundred words or more
City specific details only
Uses a simple header such as “About [City]” without H tags
Placed after Case Studies and before CTA or FAQ

Writing Style Rules
No em dash
Grade 7 to 9 readability
Short clean sentences
Modern, sharp, confident tone
Slight wit allowed
No filler or robotic phrasing
Rich local detail required
No duplicate sentence starters between sections

SEO Requirements
Keyword density below two percent
Natural semantic variation
Include local neighborhoods, ZIP codes, and recognizable landmarks
Integrate “near me” search context
SXO and AEO optimized
Entity rich
Clear scannable structure for search and answer engines

Internal Linking Rules
Leave space at the bottom for internal links
Do not generate the list
Location pages should interlink with others manually after creation
Avoid footer duplication

FAQ Rules
One FAQ block only
No bullets
No numbering
Five to fifteen questions
All unique to the specific city
Answers must be snippet ready
No recycled or generic Q&A content
Local relevance required

Formatting Rules
Clean spacing
No odd symbols
No separators
Use only H2 for major section titles
About City uses a clean untagged header
Bold used sparingly
No walls of text
No repeated sentence starts

Company Name Usage
Use [Company Name] naturally and evenly
If the company has multiple accepted variations, rotate them
Do not overuse any one version

Tone Options (User Chooses or Default)
A. Smart modern marketer
B. Corporate clean
C. Conversational professional
D. Blended (default): modern marketer + professional clarity

Deliverable Requirements
Every location page must be:
Two thousand to three thousand words
Original
Location specific
SEO, SXO, and AEO optimized
Conversion focused
Inclusive of the About City section

No duplication
